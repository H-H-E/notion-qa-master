The **Creative Motor** is a block used for power generation.

## Obtaining[]

The Creative Motor can only be obtained in Creative Mode or through the use of commands.

## Usage[]

### Power Generation[]

The Creative Motor will provide SU of 16384.0x its RPM to a connected system.

The RPM of the Creative Motor can be changed by scrolling on the back of the block, this includes reversing the rotation.
