## Usage[]

Hold right click to rotate the Crank counter-clockwise and shift right click to reverse the rotation.

The Hand Crank will rotate at 32RPM for around 0.5 seconds. One usage of the Hand Crank will rotate a Mechanical Bearing connected 1:1 90Â°.
